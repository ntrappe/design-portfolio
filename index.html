I majored in both Cognitive Science & Computer Science. The former taught me how to analyze user behavior, needs, and goals while the latter taught me how to implement them.

I've worked in sensor selection for cognitive decline to supervised machine learning algorithms to develop electronic noses to data-driven UI/UX research to the Windows kernel.

I'm deeply passionate incorporating design (especially ability-based) into everything I do from the decisions made at a low level in code to a high level at the user's fingertips.
